# Temporal Floor (the Right to Fallow Time)

The Temporal Floor guarantees every participant a baseline of **protected, unclaimed time** — a floor
on *time-to-be*, sibling to the Universal Civic Floor's floor on *resources*. Where the UCF defends
against resource-extraction, the Temporal Floor defends against **time-extraction**.

## Why this exists (three-layer grounding)

Rest here is **structural, not sentimental** — it is grounded in all three layers of the ecosystem:

- **Harmony (the principle).** In the [Constella–Harmony bridge](../../harmony/docs/constella_harmony_bridge_v1.0.0.md),
  *Yang intake* must be balanced by a *Yin return*: **"every intake needs a return path; feedforward-only
  systems drift."** A polity that is all intake — all work, obligation, and attention-capture, with no
  guaranteed return — is a feedforward-only system. It drifts and collapses, the same way a body under
  continuous load with no recovery phase fails. Fallow time *is* the civic Yin-return.
- **ALife (the receipt).** Floors raise whole-system resilience (`civic-floor` → resilience, Rust
  multi-seed; see [alife_evidence_mapping.md](alife_evidence_mapping.md)). The Temporal Floor applies the
  *same confirmed mechanism* — a guaranteed minimum that prevents collapse of the fragile — to *time*
  instead of energy. (Tier note: the sandbox confirms **floors work**; it does not and cannot measure the
  human value of silence — that is the next line.)
- **The heart (the value — asserted, per the UDHR-style values commitment).** Time to enjoy, discover,
  relax, and grow is a condition of a life worth living, not a luxury earned after productivity. Silence
  is a right. This is *envisioned, not derived* — the sandbox supplies the structure; this line supplies
  the reason.

## Core Principle

> Every verified participant is guaranteed a floor of **protected, unstructured time** — fallow time that
> **no obligation, cadence, beat, or incentive may claim.** Eligibility is existence, not merit. The floor
> is **inviolable** (it cannot be traded away, bought out, or eroded by accumulated "soft" demands) but
> **bounded** (it is a *floor*, not a ceiling — it guarantees a minimum of free time, it does not cap
> anyone's chosen work).

The parallel to the UCF is exact: the UCF is *inviolable but bounded* (funded from surplus, never inflated
past carrying capacity); the Temporal Floor is *inviolable but bounded* (a guaranteed minimum, never a
mandate to be idle).

## Mechanism

- **Fallow guarantee.** A defined minimum of each participant's time is **unschedulable** by the system:
  the [Governance Cadence](governance_cadence.md) — the polity's clock — is structurally forbidden from
  placing *any* beat (hard or offered) inside it. The cadence sets the beats; the Temporal Floor guarantees
  the silence between them.
- **Anti-accretion rule.** The floor protects against *death by a thousand soft demands* — the specific
  failure mode of the attention economy, where no single obligation is coercive but their sum consumes a
  life. Soft/offered obligations that in aggregate would breach a participant's floor are automatically
  deferred, not stacked.
- **Non-transferable.** Fallow time cannot be sold, lent, or made a condition of anything else. (A right you
  can be pressured to trade away is not a floor.)

## Boundaries (what this does NOT claim)

- The **structure** (a system needs a guaranteed return phase or it drifts) is receipt- and Harmony-backed.
  The **amount** of the floor, and the **sacredness of silence**, are *asserted values*, not sandbox
  results — set them by deliberation, not by simulation.
- It is a floor on *availability of time*, not a mandate on its *use*. A participant may spend their fallow
  time working if they freely choose to; the floor only guarantees it cannot be *taken*.
- It does not, by itself, create the *space* for restful lifestyles — that is the role of temporal/lifestyle
  niches under the Cadence (see `governance_cadence.md`, and the `niche-maintains-diversity` receipt).

## Relations

- **Sibling to the UCF** — resource floor (joules) + temporal floor (fallow time) = the two dignity floors.
- **Enforced by the Cadence** — the cadence must leave the floor fallow; that constraint is what keeps the
  clock loose (see below).
- **Grounded in Harmony** — the guaranteed Yin-return; the Cadence is the Yang↔Yin rhythm around it.
