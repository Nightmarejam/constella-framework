# Provenance

This project is **human-directed, AI-assisted** work — built through iterative
conversation ("vibe coded") and proud of it. The claim is authenticity, not
concealment, and it's verifiable:

- **Git trail**: AI-assisted commits carry `Co-Authored-By` trailers naming the
  model. Human-only commits don't. The history is the honest record.
- **Conversation corpus**: the design conversations behind this work are archived
  (privately) and processed by a documented extraction pipeline; extracted docs cite
  their source threads by title and date.
- **Confirmability tiers**: factual claims in docs carry `confirmed` / `asserted` /
  `speculative` tiers with receipts — see
  [constella-framework/docs/reference/confirmability.md](https://github.com/Nightmarejam/constella-framework/blob/main/docs/reference/confirmability.md).
  Nothing is promoted to confirmed without a checkable receipt.

Direction, decisions, hardware, testing, and the ideas are Jonathan Morales
(Nightmarejam). Drafting, review, and synthesis involve AI assistants (Claude,
GPT, local models). Errors are owned by the human; receipts let you check the work.
